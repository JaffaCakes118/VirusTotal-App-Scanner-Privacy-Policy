# Privacy Policy for VirusTotal App Scanner

**Last updated: April 29, 2026**

JaffaCakes118 ("we", "us", or "our") operates the VirusTotal App Scanner mobile application (the "Service")[cite: 3].

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data[cite: 3].

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you[cite: 3].

### Types of Data Collected

#### Package Information
To provide the core functionality of scanning your device for potential threats, the Service accesses the list of installed applications on your device[cite: 3]. Specifically:
* We collect the **Package Names** and **APK Hashes** (SHA-256) of your installed apps[cite: 3].
* **If an app's hash is not recognized by VirusTotal, the Service will upload the complete application file (APK) from your device to VirusTotal for a full security analysis.**[cite: 3]
* This information and the files are sent directly to **VirusTotal** (a third-party service) to retrieve security analysis reports[cite: 3].
* **User Consent:** The Service will explicitly request your permission via an in-app prompt before accessing your installed packages or uploading any files off your device.

#### API Keys
To use the Service, you are required to provide your own **VirusTotal API Key**[cite: 3].
* This key is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage[cite: 3].
* It is only used to authenticate your requests directly to the VirusTotal API[cite: 3].

#### Firebase Data
We use Google Firebase to enhance the Service[cite: 3]:
* **Firebase Authentication:** We use anonymous authentication to securely interact with backend services. No personal identity (like email or name) is required or collected for this[cite: 3].
* **Firebase Analytics:** We collect anonymous usage data to understand how the app is used[cite: 3].
* **Firebase Crashlytics:** We collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs and improve stability[cite: 3].

## 2. Permissions

The Service requires the following permissions to function[cite: 3]:
* **INTERNET:** Required to communicate with VirusTotal and Firebase services[cite: 3].
* **QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES:** Required to list the applications installed on your device so they can be selected for scanning[cite: 3].

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party Service Providers to facilitate our Service[cite: 3]:

* **VirusTotal:** For app security analysis. By using this app, you agree to the [VirusTotal Terms of Service](https://www.virustotal.com/en/about/terms-of-service/) and [Privacy Policy](https://www.virustotal.com/en/about/privacy/)[cite: 3].
* **Google Firebase:** For analytics, crash reporting, and backend security. You can find their Privacy Policy here: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)[cite: 3].

## 4. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

* **Local Storage:** We use a local database (Room) and Preferences (DataStore) to cache scan results and store your settings (like Dark Mode and API Key) locally on your device[cite: 3]. 
* **Data Deletion:** Because this data is stored exclusively on your local device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's cache/storage in your Android settings or by entirely uninstalling the VirusTotal App Scanner application.
* **No Developer Servers:** We do not operate our own servers[cite: 3]. Your data is never transmitted to us or stored by us.
* **Third-Party Retention:** Data sent to VirusTotal or Google Firebase is subject to their respective retention and deletion policies[cite: 3]. To request the deletion of data processed by these services, please refer to the privacy policies linked in Section 3.
